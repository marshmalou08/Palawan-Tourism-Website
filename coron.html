Coron

Wreck diving, crater lakes, and wild mountain scenery.

Coron is the principal town of Busuanga Island in the Calamian group, northeast of the Palawan mainland. It is famous worldwide for its fleet of Japanese WWII shipwrecks — sunk by American aircraft in 1944 — which now form one of the world's greatest wreck diving destinations. Above water, the landscape is equally dramatic: crystal-clear crater lakes, limestone cliffs, and pristine beaches await at every turn.

Highlights of Coron

Kayangan Lake Clearest lake in Asia
WWII Wrecks World-class wreck diving
Twin Lagoon Salt and freshwater marvel

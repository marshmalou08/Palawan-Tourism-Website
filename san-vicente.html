San Vicente

Home of Long Beach — the Philippines' longest white sand shore.

San Vicente is an emerging destination on Palawan's northwestern coast, anchored by Long Beach — a breathtaking 14-kilometre stretch of undeveloped white sand backed by palms and rolling hills. With a new airport and a growing range of accommodation, San Vicente is being developed as an ecotourism destination that aims to preserve its extraordinary natural setting while welcoming visitors who seek a quieter, less-crowded alternative to El Nido.

Highlights of San Vicente

Long Beach Fourteen kilometres of white sand
Bato ni Ningning Iconic rock on top of a hill

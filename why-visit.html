Why Visit Palawan?

Because nowhere else on Earth quite compares.

Reasons to Come

Palawan has been named the world's best island more times than any other destination. Here is why.

The Water

Palawan's lagoons, bays, and open sea offer some of the clearest, most biodiverse water in the world. From El Nido's Big and Small Lagoons to Coron's crater lakes, every body of water here is extraordinary.

The Reefs

Tubbataha Reef is one of Asia's finest dive sites and a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Coron's wrecks are among the best wreck dives on Earth. El Nido's coral gardens are accessible even to first-time snorkellers.

The Wildlife

Palawan bearcat, Philippine cockatoo, Palawan peacock-pheasant, monitor lizards, sea turtles, whale sharks — the wildlife here is extraordinary in both variety and accessibility.

The Beaches

Long Beach in San Vicente stretches 14 kilometres of undeveloped white sand. El Nido's Secret Beach is hidden behind limestone. Nacpan Beach is one of Southeast Asia's finest.

The Adventure

Island hopping, cave exploration, kayaking through mangroves, trekking to waterfalls, diving on wrecks — Palawan offers adventure at every level of intensity.

The People

Filipino hospitality is world-famous, and in Palawan it reaches its warmest expression. The communities here welcome visitors with a generosity that leaves a lasting impression.

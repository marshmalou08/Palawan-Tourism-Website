Port Barton

The quiet alternative — untouched, unhurried, unforgettable.

Port Barton is a small fishing village on Palawan's western coast, quietly earning a reputation as the island's most laid-back destination. With no ATMs, occasional electricity, and a main beach lined with simple guesthouses and rope swings, it is a place for travellers who want to slow down completely. The surrounding bay contains pristine coral reefs, mangrove-lined rivers, and islands accessible by banca boat — all without the crowds of El Nido.

Highlights of Port Barton

Coconut Beach Probably the most popular beach in Port Barton.
White Beach One of the most tranquil experiences in all of Palawan
Pamuayan Falls A quiet and peaceful waterfall

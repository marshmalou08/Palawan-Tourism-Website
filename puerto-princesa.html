Puerto Princesa

Gateway city, underground river, and culinary capital.

Puerto Princesa is the capital city of Palawan — a vibrant, well-organised city known for its cleanliness, warm hospitality, and proximity to some of the province's most remarkable natural attractions. At its heart is the Puerto Princesa Subterranean River National Park, a UNESCO World Heritage Site and one of the New Seven Wonders of Nature, where a navigable underground river winds through a cathedral of ancient limestone formations for over 8 kilometres.

Highlights of Puerto Princesa

Underground River UNESCO World Heritage Site

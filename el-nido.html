El Nido

Limestone karsts, hidden lagoons, and the finest island-hopping in Asia.

El Nido sits at the northern tip of Palawan, surrounded by a dramatic archipelago of over 50 islands and islets shaped by millions of years of limestone erosion. It is consistently ranked among the most beautiful places on Earth, drawing travellers who come for its iconic Big and Small Lagoons, the secret beach hidden behind a karst wall, and the extraordinary snorkelling and diving in its marine reserve.

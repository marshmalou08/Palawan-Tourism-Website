Budget Guide

Travelling Palawan on Any Budget

Palawan caters to every budget, from barefoot backpackers to luxury resort guests. Here is what to expect at each level.

Budget Travel

Dormitory beds in El Nido and Puerto Princesa start at PHP 400–600 per night. Fan-cooled private rooms cost PHP 800–1,500. Share van transfers, join group island-hopping tours (PHP 1,200–1,800 per person), and eat at local carinderia restaurants (PHP 80–150 per meal) for a very affordable trip.

Mid-Range

Comfortable air-conditioned guesthouses and small resorts range from PHP 2,500–5,000 per night. Private island-hopping tours can be arranged for PHP 4,000–6,000 per boat. Dining at beach restaurants costs PHP 300–600 per meal. Budget PHP 4,000–8,000 per day all-in.

Luxury

El Nido and Coron have world-class resorts ranging from PHP 15,000 to PHP 80,000+ per night. Private boat charters, exclusive island experiences, and fine dining are available. Liveaboard dive trips to Tubbataha cost USD 3,000–5,000 per week.

Money-Saving Tips

Bring enough cash — ATMs are unreliable in El Nido and absent in Port Barton. Travel shoulder season (November, May) for lower prices. Book vans and ferries directly from terminals. Buy reef-safe sunscreen before arriving — it is expensive in tourist areas.

  • Puerto Princesa → El NidoPHP 600–700 (shared van)

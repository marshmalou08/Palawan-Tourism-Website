About Us

Welcome to Discover Palawan — your guide to the last frontier.

Welcome to Discover Palawan

Discover Palawan is an independent tourism guide dedicated to showcasing the extraordinary beauty, culture, and natural heritage of Palawan province. We believe that travellers who understand and appreciate a destination's depth — its history, its people, its ecosystems — travel better, tread more lightly, and leave with experiences that last a lifetime.

Our Mission

To inspire responsible, curious, and deeply rewarding travel to Palawan.

  • Share accurate, honest, and thoughtfully written information about Palawan's destinations, culture, and natural environment.
  • Support sustainable and community-based tourism that benefits the people and ecosystems of the province.
  • Promote the extraordinary indigenous cultures of Palawan with respect and careful representation.
  • Help travellers make informed decisions that reduce their environmental footprint.
  • Celebrate the wonder of the last ecological frontier of the Philippines.

What You Can Discover

Six Destinations

Comprehensive guides to El Nido, Coron, Puerto Princesa, San Vicente, Port Barton, and Taytay — each with its own character and highlights.

Culture & Heritage

Stories of Palawan's indigenous peoples, their languages, traditions, food, and crafts — the human heritage of the last frontier.

Activities

Guides to island hopping, diving, snorkelling, kayaking, hiking, and cultural tours — adventures for every kind of traveller.

Travel Tips

Practical information on getting there, getting around, budgeting, packing, and staying safe — everything you need before you go.
